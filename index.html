Farbod Shahinfar

(Ph.D. Student)

I am a graduate student advised by Gianni Antichi at Politecnico di Milano (There is the Advanced Network Techonologies Lab there).

My field of interest is computer systems, and I have mainly focused on the intersection of Operating Systems and data-center networks. More specifically, I have explored techniques to increase performance of networked applications, including network functions, virtual switches, and more complex applications such as key-value stores and web-servers.

In this path I have explored different paradigms including network bypass techinques (e.g., DPDK, AF_XPD), and OS kernel extension using eBPF. eBPF, has been a main part of my Ph.D. studies. Specifically, I wrote a paper on its strengths and shortcomings after failing to build a general system that automatically accelerated complex socket applications.
